Hello, and welcome to my personal space. I'm Riik Basu — a PhD candidate at the Research School of Earth Sciences, Australian National University.

My research sits at the intersection of geophysics, seismology, and computational modelling. I'm drawn to the questions hidden inside the Earth's crust: what the rocks record, what the waves reveal, and what the data can teach us about the forces shaping our world.

Previously trained at IIT Kharagpur and across the IDEA League — ETH Zürich, TU Delft, and RWTH Aachen — I bring a rigorous quantitative foundation to every problem I tackle.
